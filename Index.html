How can you help

These are the steps you can take right now to help the whales.

Remind your parents not to wash your car in the driveway.

The chemicals used in washing detergents go down the storm drains, make their way to the ocean and kill fish, disturbing the delicate nature balance.

Don't eat chinook salmon.

Chinook salmon is the primary source of food for the southern resident killer whales. The number of chinook salmon has been declining. It takes a lot of time for the numbers to go back up.

Choose non-chemical elements for your yard.

Avoid pesticides and chemical fertilizers. Remember that drain water will eventually find its way into the ocean. There are better alternatives to yard care.
